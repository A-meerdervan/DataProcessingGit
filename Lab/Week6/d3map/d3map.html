A note on the choice of the colour encoding

The full data range was from 1.1 until 7.6. From the readings of this course it became clear that from 6 to about 12 color encodings can be easily visualy separeated, I chose to show more resolution around in the lower values because these are around the point of eather, going extinct or growing in population. I chose to use 9 colours to show the numbers. The color's were selected from Color Brewer
The choice was made to select two hue's (Blue and yellow) for better visual separation. The colour blue was used because and yellow are used because of it's neutral association, since I beleive large population growth is a bad thing, but large shrinking also. However Still I believe the shrinking effect to be the most negative and therefore used dark colours for this. This because it is symbolic for danger or something going the wrong way. This because a value of for example 1.2 means that the population is almost halved each generation, leading to extinction.
